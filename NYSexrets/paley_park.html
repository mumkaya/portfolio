PALEY PARK

Completed in 1984, and designed by Swanke, Hayden, and Connell, in addition to the park on the north side of East 53rd Street, just west of Madison Avenue, included a piece of the Berlin Wall. The five sections were originally located along the Waldemarstrasse and were decorated by German artists Thierry Noir and Kiddy Citny in 1985.
520 Madison Avenue
