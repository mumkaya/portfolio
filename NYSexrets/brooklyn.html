BROOKLYN SUPERHERO SUPPLY CO.

This Park Slope all-purpose superhero supply store has everything a caped crusader might need. There's a hidden doorway behind one of the bookshelves that leads to Dave Eggers's 826 NYC student literacy program, which provides one-on-one mentoring to under-resourced students ages 6-18.
